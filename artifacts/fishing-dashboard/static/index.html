💡 Oregon Tidal Fishing Tips
Rising tides push salmon and steelhead into tidal rivers (Alsea, Nestucca, Coquille). Fish estuary edges 1–2 hours before high tide. Falling tides concentrate baitfish near structure — perch, stripers, and rockfish follow. Check bar forecasts at NOAA West Coast before any bar crossing.
